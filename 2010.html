2010s

Students led a strike against the NPCSCís decision beginning on 22 September 2014, and the Hong Kong Federation of Students and Scholarism started protesting outside the government headquarters on 26 September 2014.On 28 September, events developed rapidly. The Occupy Central with Love and Peace movement announced the beginning of their civil disobedience campaign. Students and other members of the public demonstrated outside government headquarters, and some began to occupy several major city intersections.Protesters blocked both eastñwest arterial routes in northern Hong Kong Island near Admiralty. Police tactics ñ including the use of tear gas ñ and triad attacks on protesters led more citizens to join the protests and to occupy Causeway Bay and Mong Kok.
